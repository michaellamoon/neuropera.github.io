A CalArts Opera production.
An original opera that unfolds along a series of virtual neural pathways revealing stories of
despair, remembrance, justice, and hope. These stories work together in a way that weaves the
experiences of many creative minds into one potentially universal human experience.


We have curated a path through the works for the premiere that will take you through each piece to arrive at each of our three livestream works at the right time.

Abigail Whitman

Abigail Whitman is an LA-based performer,
composer, and a trained Alexander Technique
teacher. Her work explores the mind-body
relationship and how our experiences translate
into our relationship in the world. She also
teaches and performs in the LA area.

Caretha Richardson (Chizuko)

Caretha Richardson is a 22-year-old studio artist,
singer, songwriter, vocal producer, pianist, cover artist
and dancer. She makes R&B, Hip Hop and Pop music that
dives into her experiences, emotions and what goes on in the
world. Caretha also works in digital media and creates short films.

Gabriela Padilla (Gisella G)

Gabriela Padilla aka Gisella G., is a singer, songwriter,
published poet, commercial recording artist, activist,
frontwomen to band Resonance and educator. She
is also director and worship leader at her church, God’s
house of liberty.

Honiball Joseph

Honiball Joseph is a composer, conductor, and pianist,
with more than thirty five years of extensive international
experience in music and art. He is the founder and current
CEO of Gilgamesh Art & Culture Foundation where since 1986
he has been a music educator. Joseph is passionate about
helping high quality art become more accessible to the community.
The generation of new ideas through creativity and innovative
styles have been inseparable aspects of his life.

Jordyn Foley

Jordyn has performed throughout the Bay Area and Southern
California. At age 12, Jordyn competed on the "X-Factor",
and holds the title of the youngest contestant in the
show's history. Jordyn has worked in TV/Film and on various
albums. In addition to music, Jordyn teaches voice and drama
to underserved youth through Aspire and is also a character
performer for PPF, which works with groups such as the
Make-A-Wish Foundation.

Maddison Schneider

Maddison Schneider is a singer/songwriter from Sydney,
Australia. She specializes in Opera, R&B, Musical theatre
and pop, and has been performing since she was 5 years old.
She has performed in places such as The Sydney Opera House
and Dubai Opera House, and hopes to expand her musical
experiences further across the globe.

Menghe Jing

Menghe Jing is a singer, songwriter, educator from China,
Focusing on Rock & Roll, Pop, and Worship music, performed
in churches, concerts, and music festivals. Menghe commit
herself to create music to sending god’s message and inspire
more people.

Rosa Evangelina

Rosa Evangelina is a Mexican-American vocalist, poet, composer,
performance artist, and educator based in Los Angeles.
Her work investigates the performative capabilities of
voice, text, and movement, often drawing from language
and energetic traces left behind by her ancestors,
including her past self.

Simone Zapata

Simone Zapata is a poet from San Jose, CA. Her work is driven by
documentary strategies and forms that ~feel~. She
challenges the idiom “art for art’s sake” and aims to
leverage her practice as a tool for personal and
collective empowerment.

Tyler Sanders

Tyler Sanders is a musician/composer recently based in California.
Primarily writing for dance, film and music theater,
he aims to create music that keeps an audience rooted in
a sense of reality, alters that sense and transitions
between the established reality and altered sense in a
way that feels timeless.
