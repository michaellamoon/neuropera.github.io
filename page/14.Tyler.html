Project Description

This piece is part of a new series of works called Dissections of a Community. This piece, Self-Reflections of a Community as One Artist, explores the creative processes of the individuals who have worked together to curate Nueropera. The piece is broken into parts that act as neurons, designed as connective paths throughout the brain.
