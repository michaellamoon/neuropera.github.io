Project Description

This project is an exploration of the creative processes of individuals, but aims to portray them as a collective process and the individuals as a community without seeing them in a collective space. This will be achieved by threading cuts of peoples video documentation of them working on a project and having it viewed through a kaleidoscope like medium. While working, each member will have a prompt (reflections on the creative process and you as an artist) Visual concept might change
