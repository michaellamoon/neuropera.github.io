With Love, From the Universe

Rosa Evangelina

Project Collaborators

1st reading: Simone + Rosa. 2nd reading: 3 (Rosa, Jordyn, Tyler). 3rd reading: ALL

Project Description

This piece invites the performers and audience to reflect on global and personal shifts we have faced in the past year, the present moment, and what lies ahead. Simone will pull three tarot cards to illuminate each of these temporal spaces. After each reading, Rosa will lead members of the ensemble to complete the readings with vocal improvisations.
