Project Collaborators

Tarot Reading by Simone Zapata |Performed by Rosa Evangelina, Simone Zapata, Jordyn Foley, Tyler Sanders, Paul Berkolds, Carmina Escobar, Menghe Jing, Gabriela Padilla, Caretha Richardson, Maddison Schneider, Abigail Whitman, Honiball Yousef |Tech support and stage management by Madeline Falcone, Katelyn Maheia, Emilia Moscoso Borja, Olivia Popejoy

Project Description

With Love, from the Universe invites the performers and audience to reflect on global and personal shifts we have experienced in the past year, the present moment, and what lies ahead. Three tarot cards will be pulled and interpreted in the moment to illuminate each of these temporal spaces. After each card, the ensemble will complete the reading with vocal improvisations.
