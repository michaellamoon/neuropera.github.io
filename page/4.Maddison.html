Project Description

Many people struggle to give themselves a chance to allow their mind to shut off especially with the current global situation, so Maddison has created a piece that hopes to promote tranquility and relaxation for the audience. She has recorded a 5 minute improvisational song in the Dubai Desert with word placement shifting between hums and vowels harmonizing with vibrational instruments - such as singing bowls and chimes to create a meditative experience.
