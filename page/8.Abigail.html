Why We March

Abigail Whitman

Project Collaborators

Rosa and Gabriela for the duet, and Tracy for the improvisation. visual aspect: Siena Starbird

Project Description

A piece in three parts: First, an introduction, answering the question "Why do we march?" This will be simple and short, and is meant to set the scene. Then, a duet that sets the poem "Hombres necios" (Oh foolish men) by Sor Juana Ines de la Cruz. Finally, a short improvisation based off a questionnare I have written that explores the personal reasons for protesting injustices
