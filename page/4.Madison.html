Project Description

Mindfulness and self love are the main inspirations behind this project. A lot of people struggle to give themselves a chance to fully allow their mind to shut off especially with the current global situation, so I wanted this piece to promote tranquility and relaxation. I will be composing a 5 minute improvisational song with word placement shifiting between hums and vowels accompanied by vibrational instruments - such as singing bowls and chimes to create a meditative experience for the audience. I want to utilise nature, so this may be recorded in the Desert.
