Project Collaborators

Project Description

Come Sail Away is a working through of grief and familial bonds. In 2018 the artistâ€™s aunt, who was schizophrenic, died suddenly of colon cancer. Adapted from the 1977 song by Styx, Come Sail Away incorporates polyvocality, medical records and X-ray images, and tarot as testimony to the persistence of life after loss.
