Do You Hear The People Sing

Menghe Jing

Project Collaborators

Lyrics editor: Menghe Jing, Paul Berkolds; A cappella composer: Menghe Jing; Performers: Menghe Jing, Paul Berkolds, Gabriela Padilla, Tyler Sanders, Rosa Evangelina, Jordyn Foley, Maddison Schneider, Caretha Richardson; Audio mixing: Katelyn Maheia

Project Description

Do you hear the people sing is a song from Les Miserable, lyrics rewritten by Menghe Jing and Paul Berkold. This project is created to show people that musicians are still working on our area even though our stages are our own bedrooms. Everyone can fight on as well!

‘Til the virus is gone!
