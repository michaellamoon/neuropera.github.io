Project Collaborators

Project Description

This project is about the pain and suffering we have endured throughout this pandemic and how we have moved through it, with growth, and healing. Gabriela, wrote three pieces for Neuropera, called Cacophony of Healing. Enjoy this journey of songs, Pain, Acknowledgement and Healing, as you travel through the brain.
