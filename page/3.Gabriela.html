Gabriela Padilla

Project Collaborators

All class members

Project Description

This project is about the pain and suffering we have endured throughout this pandemic and how we have moved through it, grown, and healed. There will be three songs, 1)Pain 2) Aknowledgement 3) Healing and these will be viewed in a room or part of the brain central hub. "Pain" is a collaborative piece, where each collective can release and express: frustration, fear, loneliness, sadness, loss, empathy, panic, delirium, and other manifestations of the COVID pandemic. It is our intention to help facilitate healing through this piece. for song, "Aknowledgement", will need Honiball on Piano and entire class making humming and oos, ahhs vocal sounds. Last song, "Healing" will be a ballad sung by: Caretha and Tyler on piano. Pre-recorded piano and vocals. Will need Abigail, Tracy, Menghe, and Rosa on harmony and background vocals.
