Black and Brown Women

Caretha Richardson aka Chizuko

Project Collaborators

Dakiya Banks, Christine Richardson, Keya Ashimi, Cicoia Makeda, Claudia Watkins, Caretha Richardson

Project Description

This project is about the experience that Black and Brown Women have in America. It will be centered around two questions that will be asked to five different Black and Brown women. It includes different songs by Chizuko, the creator of the short film, along with original spoken word in a digital media piece.
