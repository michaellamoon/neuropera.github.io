Project Description

Inspired and modernized by the Broadway show Rent, Over the Moon discusses today's problems with gentrification and the patriarchy in a dream-like setting. In this dream world, humanity is lost and there is no love or creativity. The only way out is to jump over the moon, which is where life in general is full of community, love, and acceptance.
